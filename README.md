# Al-Masa - Premium Sweets & Bakery Mobile App 🍪🍰

**Al-Masa** is a high-end, cross-platform e-commerce application built with **Flutter**, specifically crafted for a premium sweets and bakery experience. From artisanal cookies to traditional sweets and custom cakes, Al-Masa provides a visually delightful and seamless shopping journey.

---

## ✨ Features

- **Gourmet UI/UX:** A refreshing "Mint Green" and "Cream" theme designed to highlight the freshness of bakery items.
- **Bakery Categories:** Dedicated sections for **Cookies, Traditional Sweets, Designer Cakes**, and more.
- **Smart Discovery:** Advanced search and category filtering to find your favorite treats instantly.
- **Dynamic Cart System:** Custom capsule-style quantity counter optimized for "Per Pack" or "Per Unit" sweet boxes.
- **Wishlist for Cravings:** Save your favorite sweets and desserts to order later with a single tap.
- **Premium Checkout Journey:** A smooth, step-by-step flow through Address Selection, Order Review, and Payment.
- **Real-time Order Tracking:** Stay updated on your delicious deliveries with live status tracking.

---

## 🛠️ Technical Stack

* **Frontend:** [Flutter](https://flutter.dev/) & [Dart](https://dart.dev/)
* **Architecture:** Component-based (Clean, Modular & Reusable Widgets)
* **Design Palette:** * **Primary:** Deep Forest Green (For a premium, organic touch)
  * **Secondary:** Mint & Pastel Cream (To represent freshness and bakery aesthetics)
